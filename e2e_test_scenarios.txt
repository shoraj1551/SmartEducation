# SmartEducation E2E Test Scenarios

## Scenario 1: New User Registration (Success)
1. Navigate to landing page.
2. Click "Sign Up".
3. Enter valid Name, Email, Mobile, and matching Passwords.
4. Click "Get Started".
5. Observe OTP buttons appearing for Email and Mobile.
6. Click "Verify" for Email (OTP is hardcoded to 123456).
7. Click "Verify" for Mobile (OTP is hardcoded to 123456).
8. Once both verified, click "Sign Up" button.
9. Verify redirect to `welcome.html`.
10. Complete survey flow.
11. Verify final redirect to dashboard.

## Scenario 2: New User Registration (Failures)
- **Missing Fields**: Attempt to click "Get Started" with empty fields. Verify error alerts.
- **Password Mismatch**: Enter different passwords. Verify checkmarks turn red or error message.
- **Duplicate Email**: Attempt registration with an already registered email. Verify error from backend.
- **Invalid OTP**: Enter wrong OTP code. Verify error message.

## Scenario 3: Login (Existing User)
1. Navigate to landing page.
2. Click "Sign In".
3. Toggle to "Email" or "Mobile".
4. Enter correct credentials.
5. Click "Login".
6. Verify redirect to dashboard.

## Scenario 4: Forgot Password Flow
1. Navigate to landing page.
2. Click "Sign In" -> "Forgot Password?".
3. Enter registered Email/Mobile.
4. Click "Send OTP".
5. Enter OTP (123456).
6. Enter new password and confirm.
7. Click "Reset Password".
8. Verify success message and try login with new password.
